About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Geospatial Computer Science program at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi. I hold a bachelor’s degree in Geographic Information Science (minor in Business Administration) and a master’s in Geospatial Systems Engineering from Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi ('20, '22). I have experience in web app development, visualization, and analysis of geospatial data, and implementation of GNSS and UAS-SfM photogrammetry workflows for optimal data acquisition and processing. My current work focuses on reality capture technologies, GNSS, and GeoAI techniques for data fusion and the generation of digital twins of built and natural environments.
